About Me

Audrey Van Leer is an aspiring professional character designer who's grown up and lives in San Jose, California. Art has mostly been a hobby for them but they've come to the realization that they can and want to make a career out of it. Fascinated by characters in stories, games, shows, movies, and characters made by their peers, they are inspired to design characters. They find character design very important as it draws in an audience. They usually draw casually with pencil and paper but are excited about exploring new mediums. They currently attend San Jose State University to help them elevate their art.
