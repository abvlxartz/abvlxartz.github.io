Beckett Van Leer's Art


Hello, I'm Beckett. This portfolio showcases my work from my freshman year of San Jose State University. I took Digital Media art (art 74) and Intro to Digital Video Art (Art 75) my freshman year. Within that year I learned the basics of photoshop, illustrator, zbrush, blender, audacity, premier pro, and audition.



This piece was a self portrait collage made in photoshop. I wanted to convey an outer space and underwater theme hence the moon and water pattern with jellyfish.
