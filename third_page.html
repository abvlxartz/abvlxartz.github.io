Meme Mashup


These two pieces were created by using photoshop. We used images of preexisting memes from knowyourmemes.com and combined them with untreated pictures. I used the popular Twilight meme template, "Bella, where the hell have you been Loca", and inserted Mikey Mouse, because Jacob's stance reminds me of Mickey Mouse dancing, and an edited tag line. For the one with the grinch I combined it with the "you wot mate" meme but edited the text out of it.
