Blender Environment

The brief for this project was to sculpt a landscape in blender. My goal for this project was to have a stone head with the side exposed to show a smaller environment. I planned for there to be a wooden door, stone steps, a pond, a waterfall, and a tree. I sculpted the head in zbrush and imported it into blender. Upon applying a stone texture to the zbrush sculpt, it turned bright magenta and didn't adopt the stone texture.
