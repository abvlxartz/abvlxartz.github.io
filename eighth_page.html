Short films


This piece was was one of the first projects for this class. The goal of the project was to explore the basics of premier pro such as importing our own video clips and using transitions before adding audio.


This piece put all the skills we learned: importing video clips, editing and splicing video clips, resizing.cropping video clips, editing the timing and pacing of the video clips and how to import and edit audio. The artistic focus of this piece is to show what it's like grappling with emotions when you're alone: in the dark.
